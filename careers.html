Join Our Team

We're always open for talented individuals to join our team and build amazing products together. At Techgaun Nepal, you will be involved in wide spectrum of projects and technologies. We primarily focus on three main facets: our own product development, open source projects primarily for Nepal, and highest quality consulting and development services for clients.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation embracing new ideas and creative solutions while fostering an environment that encourages experimentation.
  • Excellence maintaining high standards of work and continuous improvement, combined with an enthusiastic approach to challenges.
  • Ownership taking responsibility and initiative in one’s work, encouraging a sense of accountability and being proactive and accountable for results.
  • Playfulness embracing a playful attitude towards work and finding joy in the process of creating and innovating in light-hearted and dynamic atmosphere.
